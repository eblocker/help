What are Domain and Pattern Blockers?

The Domain Blockers for ads and trackers already block when calling the domain, ads and trackers. These blockers can also be used without activating the eBlocker HTTPS function.

The Pattern Blockers for ads and trackers recognize the trackers and ads by using patterns. For these blockers the activation of the eBlocker HTTPS function is necessary.
