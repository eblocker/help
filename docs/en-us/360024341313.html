Does the eBlocker continue to work (even without updates)?

The eBlocker will continue to work without updates, but the full scope of protection may not be available after some time, as new/changed trackers and malware are potentially no longer detected. A final update is planned for June, so that eBlocker customers can use public, or free list sources for filters.
