Check the network connection with macOS.

If you do not know your eBlocker's IP address, please check the network settings of your router. There you should be able see the eBlocker and its IP address.

  • Please launch the macOS Network Utility (you can do this through macOS Spotlight by typing Network Utility)
  • Click Traceroute
  • Enter the domain name: www.eblocker.com
  • Click Trace

This traceroute shows you the way from your Mac through the Internet to our website. Your eBlockers IP address should be in the first place followed by your routers IP address.
