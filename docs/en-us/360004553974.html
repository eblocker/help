DIY eBlocker - Supported Hardware

Technically experienced users can assemble their own eBlocker (DIY - Do It Yourself). Simply download the eBlockerOS for free, request a free 30-day trial license and surf anonymously immediately. All you need is a Raspberry Pi or Banana Pi and an 8Gb Class 10 SD card.

The eBlockerOS currently supports:

  • Raspberry Pi 2 version 1.1 and version 1.2
  • Raspberry Pi 3
  • Raspberry Pi 3 B+
  • Banana Pi M2
  • Banana Pi M2+

Important: Make sure that your power supply delivers 10 watts or 2A. Most standard USB power supplies for computers and routers do not provide this. This can lead to problems and errors under certain circumstances.

The eBlockerOS and the free 30 day demo license are available here.
