Where can I find the required OpenVPN files for the eBlocker?

Below you will find the paths to the proper OpenVPN files of some selected providers.
It is important that you have an account with the VPN provider, because without this the VPN connections will not work.


Perfect Privacy

Login > Download > Select the Router-Icon > Click on the "Show" button at "eBlocker" > Then click on the "Download" button

VyprVPN

https://support.goldenfrog.com/hc/en-us/articles/225607167-Where-can-I-find-the-OpenVPN-files

NordVPN

https://downloads.nordcdn.com/configs/archives/servers/ovpn.zip
Use the UDP files.

SaferVPN

https://www.safervpn.com/support/articles/214036025-What-are-SaferVPN-s-OpenVPN-configuration-files-for-manual-setup

Use the UDP files.

ProtonVPN / ProtonVPN Free (Japan / Netherlands / USA)

Login > Download > Select Linux > Select UPD > Download Server Configs

ExpressVPN

  • Login > Active Subscriptions > Click on "Set Up ExpressVPN" > "Manual Config" on the left side > select "OpenVPN".
  • There you will see your username and password which are important to setup the connections.
  • Download one of the OpenVPN configurations.
  • Important: Scroll down to "4. Optional" and download the "ZIP file" which will contain your VPN certificates and keys.

Hide.me (none free account)

Login > Servers > Download one of the locations

CyberGhost

  • Login > My Devices > click on "Other" to add a new device > click on the button "create new credentials"
  • Click on the new device and give it a name like "eBlocker".
  • Keep all settings disabled.
  • Select the "OpenVPN (UDP)" protocol
  • Select the country
  • You will need the generated "Username" and the generated "Password"
  • Click on "Download Configuration"

PureVPN

Download the VPN configuration here: https://s3-us-west-1.amazonaws.com/heartbleed/linux/linux-files.zip
Use the UDP configurations.

IPVanish

Download the VPN configuration here: https://www.ipvanish.com/software/configs/configs.zip

Private Internet Access

Download the VPN configurations here: https://deu.privateinternetaccess.com/openvpn/openvpn.zip

StrongVPN

  • Login > click on "VPN Accounts" tab and select "VPN Accounts Summary "
  • Click on the "VPN "Account Setup Instructions" Button
  • You will need the "Login" and "Password" information
  • Scroll down and click at Manual setup on the "OpenVPN" Button
  • Select your preferred country and city and select UDP
  • Click on the button "Get OpenVPN configuration"
