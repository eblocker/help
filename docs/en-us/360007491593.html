Tips for the eBlocker Dashboard

The eBlocker Dashboard is the control center for your device and your eBlocker. Here you can view all important information and make changes.

Gradually we will make more and more so-called cards available in the dashboard and thus greatly expand the range of functions. You will also be able to do more complex configurations of eBlocker for your device via the dashboard.

You can open the dashboard with the URL eblocker.box or via the website setup.eblocker.com.Â  You can also enter the IP address of your eBlocker and write /dashboard behind it ( example: 192.168.10.210/dashboard ).

Tip: Create a bookmark for your eBlocker Dashboard in your browser or even better, set the Dashboard as the start page for your browser - so you always have all information quickly at hand.

Firefox - Bookmark eBlocker Dashboard:
Open the eBlocker Dashboard and click on the three dots in the address bar on the right. Select "Bookmark This Page".

Firefox - Make eBlocker Dashboard your homepage:
Open the Firefox preferences and select "Home". Select "Custom URLs" from the "Home page and new windows" drop-down menu and enter the address of your eBlocker Dashboard.

Chrome - Bookmark eBlocker Dashboard:
Open the eBlocker Dashboard and click on the star in the address bar on the right side of your browser. Bookmark the eBlocker Dashboard there.

Chrome - Make eBlocker Dashboard your homepage:
Open the Chrome settings and then the settings. Select "On Startup" from the menu. Now select "Specific page or set of pages" and enter the address of your eBlocker Dashboards.

Opera - Bookmark eBlocker Dashboard:
Open the eBlocker Dashboard and click on the heart in the address bar on the right. Bookmark your eBlocker Dashboard there.

Opera - Make eBlocker Dashboard your homepage:
Open the Opera settings and then click on "Browser" in the menu. Select at "On Startup" the option "Open specific page or set of pages" and enter the address of your eBlocker Dashboard.

Windows Edge - Bookmark eBlocker Dashboard:
Open the eBlocker Dashboard and click on the star in the address bar on the right side of your browser. Bookmark your eBlocker Dashboard there.

Windows Edge - Set eBlocker Dashboard as your home page:
Open Edge Settings and select "A specific page or pages" from the "Open Microsoft Edgy with" drop-down menu. Here you enter the address of your eBlocker Dashboard.

Windows Internet Explorer - Bookmark eBlocker Dashboard:
Open the eBlocker Dashboard and click on the star in the address bar on the right side of your browser. Now click on "Add to favorites" and bookmark the eBlocker overview.

Windows Internet Explorer - Make eBlocker Dashboard your homepage:
Open the Internet Explorer settings, then click on "Internet Options" and select the "General" tab. Enter the address of your eBlolcker Dashboard in the Home page text field.
