Issues with the Fritzbox 7490 / 7360 / 3490 / 3272

These Fritzbox models are only compatible with your eBlocker if you deactivate the DHCP server and configure your eBlocker individually, as well as use the DHCP server of your eBlocker. Please go to our knowledge base to find out how to configure your eBlocker individually.
